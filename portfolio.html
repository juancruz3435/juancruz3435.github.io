University of Calgary Solar Car Team

I joined the solar car team's electrical department at the begining of the 2021/2022 school year. I have worked on different types of projects ranging from Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design to battery testing systems.


Projects

Recently I have completed designing and assembling a Control Area Network (CAN) to a DB9 port adapter. This Printed Circuit Board (PCB) will work as a bridge from the CAN lines in the car to a DB9 connection on a laptop.
- Skills I aquired from this project:
  • • How to use Altium Designer.
  • • Operate a soldering iron and a hot air station.
  • • Good soldering practices.
  • • Calculating the required component values.
- Key take aways:
  • • Utilzing industry standard software like Altium designer to create PCBs.
  • • Implement a reverse polarity protection circuit.
